Motion & Interaction

How should things move? This sets the personality — every hover, transition, and page change.

A

Precise & Snappy

Fast transitions (150-200ms). Spring physics with low bounce. Hover states are instant. Page transitions are a quick fade+slide (200ms). Feels like a responsive instrument — every click has immediate feedback. Think: Linear, Vercel dashboard.

Easing: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)
Duration: 120-250ms
Spring: stiffness 400, damping 30
B

Fluid & Graceful

Smooth transitions (300-500ms). Elements ease in with deceleration. Page transitions have a gentle fade+scale. Hover states bloom softly. Feels like things are breathing — alive but calm. Think: Apple's interfaces, Stripe.

Easing: cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
Duration: 250-500ms
Spring: stiffness 150, damping 20
C

Cinematic & Theatrical

Staggered entrance animations. Cards cascade in sequence. Numbers count up. Page transitions are choreographed (element-by-element). Feels like the UI is performing for you. Think: Iron Man HUD, Minority Report.

Easing: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)
Duration: 300-800ms, staggered 50-80ms per item
Spring: stiffness 200, damping 25
